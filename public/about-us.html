About Us

a note from the founders

Finding and acquiring a style that perfectly suited us had to be considered an extensive, lengthy journey—one that required so much time and effort. Indeed, having to experiment with numerous makeup and fashion styles ended up being frustrating and exhausting. A moment that often occurs in our lives was to find other styles looking great on other people, but it devastatingly wouldn’t go as expected when it came to trying them on ourselves.

And with this obstacle, it deeply ignited a burning desire within us: to simplify the entire procedure of figuring out what truly fits oneself—the birth of Amélioré.

Amélioré truly elevates one’s beauty to the maximum. Given that every single individual possesses their own distinct characteristics, Amélioré makes its way to their perfection, offering personalized solutions that highlight one’s uniqueness and individuality.

Agile, attainable, and aspiring. That’s what Amélioré is.
